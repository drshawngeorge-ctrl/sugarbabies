# Neonatal Glucose Surveillance

Purpose:
Identify infants requiring asymptomatic glucose surveillance during the first 72 hours of life.

Primary feature:
Birthweight percentile calculator using Canadian Birth Weight for Gestational Age reference.

Requirements:
- GA weeks + days
- Sex
- Birthweight
- Diabetes
- Maternal beta-blocker
- IUGR
- Asphyxia
- Antenatal steroids

Output:
- Estimated percentile
- SGA/AGA/LGA
- Screening recommendation
- PSBC surveillance duration
- Copy Brief Note
- Copy Full Assessment

PSBC Durations:
IDM 12h
LGA 12h
Maternal beta-blocker 12h
SGA 24h
Preterm 24h
IUGR 24h
Asphyxia 24h
Antenatal steroids 24h

Exclude:
- Symptomatic hypoglycemia
- Persistent hypoglycemia >72h
- NICU individualized management
- Suspected endocrine/metabolic disease
